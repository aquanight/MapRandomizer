Speed Booster

This affects the Speed Booster item.

  • Vanilla: Speed Booster is a single collectible item and functions identically to the vanilla game.
  • Split: Speed Booster is replaced with two new collectible items, "Spark Booster" and "Blue Booster".
    • Blue Booster allows Samus to run, gain blue, and jump at high speeds, but not to shinespark.
    • Spark Booster allows Samus to shinespark, but not to jump with blue or high speed.
    • Either item allows Samus to run through enemies, Speed blocks, and bomb blocks.

This setting is taken into account in the logic.
